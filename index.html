Our speakers


Satish Babu

Satish Babu is a Free Software activist, early Internet advocate, and development professional based out of Kerala, India.He has several achievements to his credit such as being the Founding Director of ICFOSS, the Chair of IEEE Kerala Section, the Chairman of Internet Society, Trivandrum Chapter.

He was earlier the CEO of SIFFS, an NGO of small-scale artisanal fishers of south India. He is also the Founder and President of InApp.Hear him speak about the Internet, it's democracy and the role engineers can play

Balasankar "Balu" C

Balasankar works remotely as a distribution engineer in Gitlab and is also an active Debian developer.His passion for the free and open source has led to contributions over the last 8 yaers to noteworthy organizations- Mozilla,GNOME to name a few.

A man of strong beliefs ,Balu is determined to spread the word of digital freedom and open goodness to nuture contributors of tomorrow

Amarjit Singh

Amarjit Singh is a freelance consultant and software developer with special interests in community engangement ,training,and mentoring. His passion for promoting FOSS,creating and bonding led him to take the initiative in expanding the scale of FOSSMEET of NITC to what it is now.

An energetic speaker enthusiastic about giving back to the society,his zeal can enliven any audience.

Ejaz Mirzan

Ejaz Mirzan currently works as a physical design engineer at intel.He has recieved several awards for his work such as ILP kudos award,a testimony to hispassion and dedication.

A passionate speaker ever ready to give back to his alma mater,explore the fields of embedded systems,python development and VLSI with him.
